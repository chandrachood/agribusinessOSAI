Agri Business Os AI

The Multilingual Agentic Ecosystem for Precision Agribusiness


Overview

Agri Business Os AI is a proprietary, end-to-end intelligence platform designed to transform raw farm data into high-yield commercial roadmaps. 
By leveraging Google Gemini and the Google Agent Development Kit (ADK), the system orchestrates specialized AI agents to solve the 
“What, When, and How” of modern farming.

Designed for regional inclusivity, the platform supports seamless interaction in Malayalam, Hindi, and English, ensuring that advanced agricultural 
intelligence is accessible to every stakeholder.


Core Modules

1. The Roadmap Engine (Agentic AI)
   A sophisticated interface where users input land metrics, tool availability, soil profiles, capital, and geo-location.

The Output:
A comprehensive, step-by-step Agribusiness Roadmap providing crop selection, optimal planting windows, and resource optimization strategies to maximize ROI.


2. Gov-Scheme Discovery & Search
   A dedicated agent that scans local and national databases to identify:

* Subsidies and grants based on land size and crop choice
* Government support through real-time discovery of schemes farmers can access immediately


3. Integrated Video Ecosystem
   A curated library connecting farmers with specialized content creators.

 Expert guidance through educational content on sustainable practices and tool usage
 Community knowledge bridging the gap between agricultural influencers and field operations


Technical Architecture

* Orchestration: Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) for multi-agent reasoning
* Intelligence: Google Gemini (multimodal and multilingual processing)
* Hosting: Google Cloud Run (serverless, auto-scaling, cloud-native)
* Backend: Python-based microservices architecture
* Future Expansion: Native mobile application (iOS and Android)
